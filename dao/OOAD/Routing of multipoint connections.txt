name: Routing of multipoint connectionsurl: http://www.researchgate.net/publication/3232524_Routing_of_multipoint_connectionspublic_in: IEEE Journal on Selected Areas in Communicationsauthors: WAXMAN,B. M.,abstract: The author addresses the problem of routing connections in a large-scale packet-switched network supporting multipoint communications. He gives a formal definition of several versions of the multipoint problem, including both static and dynamic versions. He looks at the Steiner tree problem as an example of the static problem and considers the experimental performance of two approximation algorithms for this problem. A weighted greedy algorithm is considered for a version of the dynamic problem which allows endpoints to come and go during the life of a connection.
