name: Overview of the H.264/AVC video coding standardurl: http://www.emeraldinsight.com/servlet/linkout?suffix=b34&amp;dbid=16&amp;doi=10.1108%2F17427371111123676&amp;key=10.1109%2FTCSVT.2003.815165public_in: IEEE Transactions on Circuits and Systems for Video Technologyauthors: Wiegand, T., Sullivan, G.J., Bjontegaard, G.,abstract: H.264/AVC is newest video coding standard of the ITU-T Video  Coding Experts Group and the ISO/IEC Moving Picture Experts Group. The  main goals of the H.264/AVC standardization effort have been enhanced compression performance and provision of a &quot;network-friendly&quot; video representation addressing &quot;conversational&quot; (video telephony) and &quot;nonconversational&quot; (storage, broadcast, or streaming) applications. H.264/AVC has achieved a significant improvement in rate-distortion efficiency relative to existing standards.
