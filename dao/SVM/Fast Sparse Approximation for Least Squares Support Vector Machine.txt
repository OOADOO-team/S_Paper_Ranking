FSALS-SVM iteratively builds the decision function by,adding one basis function from a kernel-based dictionary at one time. The process is terminated by using a flexible and stable epsilon insensitive stopping criterion. A probabilistic speedup scheme is employed to further improve the speed of FSALS-SVM and the resulting classifier is named PFSALS-SVM. Our algorithms are of two compelling features: low complexity and sparse solution.
